Coming soon: Flying Simulator 2009

Get on board and fly the modern passenger planes of the world. This is by far the most realistic flight simulator ever made, with events happening real-time according to detailed statistical analysis.
  • Fly between almost all airports in the world, over 9000 will be available in the first version(data delivered by ICAO)
  • Slick flying interface made in Adobe Flash, with unprecedented graphics and realism
  • Tight integration with Google maps for keeping track of your itenary and previous flights
  • Earn frequent flier miles for your flights and compete with other persons online
  • Actual weather from around the world integrated into simulation provide ground breaking experience
  • Gather flags from around the world as you visit the most remote airports on earth. Can you collect them all?
  • All aspects of flying are covered, you would swear that you are on the inside of a plane

This project is currently in a prototyping phase, and more information will follow
